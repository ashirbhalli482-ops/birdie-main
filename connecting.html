Overview of Connections

By providing solutions specifically designed for social care agencies like yours, we hope to significantly minimise the amount of time spent on finance and rostering by care agencies. Our tools were co-developed with finance and care specialists.

Family App

When they can't, we're here.

Care is, as we all know, a team effort. We searched for ways to enhance how you communicate with the rest of your extended care circle because of this. We have the resources to connect with everyone who needs to be informed, whether it's updating family members or coordinating treatment with a nearby GP.

Offer families reassurance Family frequently play a significant role. Now they will be aware of everything as it happens, from physical health and daily mood to medication and food intake.

Delete your transfer emails. Sharing thoughts with those who matter most is now simple thanks to Birdie. You may provide transparent, real-time updates with a few clicks, letting the family members know exactly what care has been given to their loved one.

Indeed, it's free! We are aware of the damage that worrying can cause. We created the Family App free for all family members so they could have live updates and rest easy knowing their loved ones were receiving the best care.
